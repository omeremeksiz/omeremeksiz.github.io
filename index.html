Research
A Reinforcement Learning Model for Industrial Filling Process Control
Ömer Sabri Emeksiz, Mert Eren Ağcabay, Engin Maşazade Suat Selim, Taner Boysan
IEEE ICECS, 2023

This paper proposes a reinforcement learning-based industrial filling process to address limitations of PID controllers in practical cases, such as discrete action spaces, residual material overflow, and material properties affecting flow. By recording episodes, defining state-action pairs, and using rewards and penalties, the Monte Carlo method optimizes actions to minimize filling duration.
